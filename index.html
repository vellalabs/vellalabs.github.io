Platform approach

Modular building blocks that scale with your greenhouse

Core modules

  • Edge collectors for sensor ingestion and local safety controls
  • Cloud telemetry for durable time-series storage and analytics
  • Dashboards for real-time monitoring, alerts, and historical views
  • Model services for prediction, anomaly detection, and optimization
  • Actuation layer for pumps, valves, fans, vents, and lighting control

Design principles

  • Safety-first: interlocks, manual override, and conservative defaults
  • Observability: logs, metrics, tracing, and sensor health checks
  • Reproducibility: experiment tracking and versioned configurations
  • Modularity: swap sensors/models without redesigning everything
  • Scale-ready: from pilot unit to multi-acre operations
